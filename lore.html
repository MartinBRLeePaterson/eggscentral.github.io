TASTY LOCATIONS

This is what we, EGGS, have managed to piece together from intel gathered from spies and TASTY agents.

Alcatraz-Their interrogation/prison facilities.
    We don't have a lot of intel on this. We discovered it by accident when Hatch was taken prisoner and we tracked her there, and she doesn't want to speak about it anytime soon.

Brainwashers-Where they train the new recruits. They call it the Institute.
    They have barracks at KG5, German Swiss International, an unknown Woodlands preschool and at Glenealy. There may be more, but we don't have confirmation. Glenealy is the biggest barracks, as it's built on a humongous mountain-lots of earth to tunnel under! 

The Factory Farms-Where did you think they got their meat?
    Think of a factory farm. Now multiply the stink, noise, filth, crowd and all-round terror and you have your basic TASTY farm. They are strategically placed under schools in the countryside, with less pipelines and wires running underground. Tiny fist-sized holes allow the bare minimum of vitamin D to be produced. The life expectancy for animals in one of these factories is 40% lower than the average.

Fund-raising Stations-Where they get their funds.
    Every. Single. Fast. Food. Shop. In. The. World.

Headquarters-Every organization has one.
    Every TASTY agent has rather died than give this away, but we have one clue: the last words forced out of an agent were "It's at S-" and then anti-climatically, he died.
