GADGETS

Eggs Radio:
It's disguised as an eraser to avoid detection. It sends messages on an unused frequency unique to EGGS. Invented by Fritz Simon in 1971.

La Tele-Puerta
Walking is for noobs, but there are restrictions on using this device as never walking tends to make people overweight. Invented by Link Gordon in 2000, who enjoys electronic engineering and quantum physics.

Steganography Pen
The top unscrews. There's a hollow section you can slip paper into.

VEHICLES

The Persephone Probes

Persephone 1 has a Vantablack outer coat to make it pretty much invisible in space, while Persephone 2 has a mirrored outer coat, ideal for stealthy planetary exploration. Both shuttles are shaped like sideways eggs, with fins for descent and ascent. They both dock at the Demeter I, but arerarely not in use.
