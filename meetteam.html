Plesiosaur-EGGS
Martin G. Adjet's not human. He comes from Atlantis (yes, underwater, real, get over it), and has basic psychic and shapeshifting powers. Weird enough for you? He's also an eccentric inventor with an android dog. He's the rebellious son of an Atlantean Senator, who ran away to Earth in the spacepod he got for his birthday.

Stegosauruswhalepotato-EGGS
Stegosauruswhalepotato, king of potatoes rules the kitchen. When he's not cooking, he's attempting to start conversations with his plants, Arthur, Merlin and Gwen. Just don't ask him about the lance. That story should never be remembered.
